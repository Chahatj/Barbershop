About Hardik Parlour

20 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE IN HAIR CUT! We offer a wide variety of services that will certainly make you the talk of the town. Our unparalleled team of expert barbers has personal pride riding on each haircut and hot lather shave. No one on Hardik Parlour Barbers’ staff has been cutting hair for less than 10 years.
